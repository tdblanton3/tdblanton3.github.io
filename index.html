About

Driven analytical professional skilled in using a specific set of tools, such as PostgreSQL, Tableau, Excel, PowerPoint, and Python, to analyze and extract business insights that deliver profitability, growth, and productivity. Proven success in developing relationships and working closely with others to provide problem-solving results in a timely manner. My name is Travis and I am, and will always be, a fighting Texas Aggie! Yes I am one of those. I was born and raised a Texan and have always found a passion working with computers. I built my first homemade computer when I was 14, and grew up helping my dad in his business, Broadcast Fusion llc., where I built and upgraded X-files and IP Directors, build all kinds of cables, set up networks, and was lucky enough to travel to the NBA Finals, Winter and Summer X-games, and The ESPY's to help upgrade their storage systems. After I graduated high school I continued to work with my dad remotely while attending Texas A&M University. My dad got a contract with CBS NFL, so every Thursday through Sunday we would remote in to all the CBS trucks at each site and setup a storage system to capture all the individual clips from each camera feed of each game, and then we would send them directly to New York where they could use the clips later or immediately for highlight reels and other purposes. It came with its challenges but it was an amazing opportunity that I got to embrace! After college I went on to work at Baylor Scott & White in Frisco where I watch heart rhythms on the Post-Surgical Unit, communicate with doctors and nurses to get orders into the system, and also work as a patient care tech. Being the computer savy guy I am I also took on the role of being the on floor IT guy and anytime there were any computer problems they would call me before the IT department. I am currently working towards a full time position in the Data Analyst field and would love to connect if you need someone to go above and beyond for you!
